About Phishly

üêü Phishly

A phishing simulation platform for security awareness training

Phishly is a comprehensive security awareness training platform designed to help organizations test and improve their employees' ability to recognize and respond to phishing attacks. Through controlled simulations, administrators can create realistic phishing campaigns, track employee interactions, and analyze security awareness metrics.

The platform enables organizations to proactively identify vulnerabilities in their human firewall and provide targeted training to reduce the risk of successful phishing attacks.
